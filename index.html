My Work

Kitchen Design & Rendering

A modern kitchen visualization that communicates layout, finishes, and lighting before build. The concept balances workflow and storage with a clean, warm palette clients can relate to. Lighting is layered (task + ambient) for accurate previews of daytime and evening scenes. Cabinet modules and clearances are arranged to minimize door conflicts and tight corners. Deliverables include plan, elevations, and render views to speed alignment with installers.

Kitchen Design

A functional concept emphasizing circulation, work triangle efficiency, and accessible storage. Neutral materials keep the space timeless while allowing easy appliance or color updates later. Drawer stacks replace deep shelves for faster access to cookware and daily essentials. Tall pantry placement shortens travel distance between prep, cook, and serve zones. Documentation focuses on practical dimensioning that reduces on-site guesswork.

Living Room & Dining Interior

Open-plan living/dining arranged for clear movement and natural sightlines across the space. Seating and table positions support conversation while maintaining comfortable walk paths. Materials and textures are selected for durability and easy upkeep in daily use. Lighting scenes separate lounge, dining, and ambient moods without visual clutter. The result is a calm, modern environment that photographs well and feels welcoming in person.
